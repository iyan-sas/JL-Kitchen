About Us

Our Story

A daring concept emerged in the center of San Fernando, Pampanga, which is regarded as the Philippines' culinary capital: to establish a location where anyone could freely savor one of the most famous Kapampangan dishes. The founders, a family of enthusiastic home cooks, had spent years honing their own lechon recipe, experimenting with slow-roasting methods, traditional Kapampangan flavors, and generation-old secret marinades. What began as a modest weekend roasting company soon became a local favorite. After tasting their lechon, locals would frequently return and joke, "Dapat gawin yu nang unli'to!" Motivated by the increasing demand, the family saw an opportunity to launch the first unlimited lechon dining experience in San Fernando.

Our Mission

Our goal at JL Kitchen's is to provide freshly made, premium, reasonably priced unli lechon that fulfills every desire. We are dedicated to providing consistently delicious food, guaranteeing client satisfaction, and fostering a friendly atmosphere where everyone can savor delicious meals, excellent deals, and wonderful moments.

Our Vision

To become the most reputable and cherished source of unlimited lechon in San Fernando, renowned for its superb flavor, friendly service, and communal dining experience.

Our Values

Freshness: We roast our lechon daily to ensure the crispiest skin and most tender meat.
Quality: We source only the finest ingredients.
Customer Satisfaction: Your happiness is our success.
Celebration: Every meal should be a fiesta!
